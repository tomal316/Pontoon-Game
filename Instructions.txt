Compile and Run Instruction:

1) Open Terminal/Console/Command Prompt
2) Type in javac *.java. (This will compile the code)
3) Enter java Driver. (This is for running the program)

How to play:

1) When you see the frame, click 'New Game' Button. This will start the game.
2) Follow the instructions, on whose turn it is.
3) Click on the numbers on your turn.
4) The aim is to keep the total below 22. The first player to take the total above
     21 will loose and the winner will be declared.
5) Click on 'New Game' button to restart the game at any point in the game.
